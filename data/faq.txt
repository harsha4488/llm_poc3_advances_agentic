COMPANY POLICIES AND SUPPORT KNOWLEDGE BASE

REFUND POLICY
Refunds are processed within 5 business days after the returned item is received.
Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
Refunds are not available for digital products.
Refund requests must be initiated within 14 days of delivery.
If a refund is delayed beyond 7 business days, customers should contact support.

ORDER PROCESSING
Orders are processed within 24 to 48 hours after placement.
Orders placed on weekends are processed on the next business day.
Customers receive a confirmation email once the order is processed.
Orders cannot be modified once shipped.

SHIPPING INFORMATION
Standard shipping takes 3 to 5 business days.
Express shipping takes 1 to 2 business days.
Shipping delays may occur due to weather or customs inspections.
International shipping may take up to 10 business days.
Tracking information is shared via email once the order ships.

ORDER STATUS
Order status can be checked using the order ID.
Statuses include: Processing, Shipped, Out for Delivery, Delivered.
If an order is marked delivered but not received, customers must report it within 48 hours.
Lost orders are investigated within 5 business days.

CANCELLATION POLICY
Orders can be cancelled only if they have not been shipped.
Cancellation requests must be made within 12 hours of order placement.
Once cancelled, refunds are processed within 3 business days.
Partially shipped orders cannot be cancelled.

PAYMENT ISSUES
Failed payments may occur due to insufficient balance or bank restrictions.
Duplicate charges should be reported immediately.
Payment disputes are resolved within 7 business days.
Only supported payment methods are eligible for refunds.

ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT
Users can update their profile information at any time.
Passwords must contain at least 8 characters.
Accounts locked due to suspicious activity can be recovered via email verification.
Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

CUSTOMER SUPPORT
Customer support operates Monday to Friday from 9 AM to 6 PM.
Support tickets are prioritized based on severity.
High priority issues are addressed within 4 hours.
Support is unavailable on public holidays.

RETURNS
Items must be returned in original packaging.
Damaged items must be reported within 24 hours of delivery.
Return shipping costs are covered only for defective products.
Return approvals are valid for 7 days.

SECURITY AND COMPLIANCE
All user data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
Access to sensitive data is logged and audited.
Security incidents are investigated within 24 hours.
The system complies with GDPR and internal security standards.

ESCALATION POLICY
Issues unresolved after 2 attempts are escalated to a senior agent.
Billing disputes above a defined threshold require manager approval.
Security-related issues are immediately escalated.
Low-confidence automated responses must be reviewed by a human agent.
